CAO is a simple “spot the difference” game that was developed by an independent game studio based in Vietnam. (Nah, not really, we do not actually have a studio). There are 630 stages in the game that represent beautiful landscapes throughout 63 geographical regions in Vietnam. Our goal is to honor Vietnamese stunning sceneries and to promote tourism to locals and foreigners.
