CAO is a “spot the difference” mobile game that was developed by an independent game studio based in Vietnam. (Nah, not really, we do not actually have a studio). There are 630 stages in the game that represent beautiful landscapes throughout 63 geographical regions in Vietnam. Our goal is to honor Vietnamese stunning sceneries and to promote tourism to locals and foreigners.

On this website, you can learn more about the faces behind the game, check out some Vietnamese sceneries, and try our game in demo mood.
